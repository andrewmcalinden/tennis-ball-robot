
Tennis - v8 2021-10-17 12:02pm
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on October 17, 2021 at 5:59 PM GMT

It includes 553 images.
Tennis-balls are annotated in COCO format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 640x480 (Fit (black edges))

The following augmentation was applied to create 5 versions of each source image:
* Random brigthness adjustment of between -23 and +23 percent
* Random exposure adjustment of between -31 and +31 percent
* Random Gaussian blur of between 0 and 3.25 pixels
* Salt and pepper noise was applied to 2 percent of pixels
